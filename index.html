WHO WE ARE

Ontario Tech Racing is a team of 70 passionate engineering and business students designing, manufacturing, and marketing a Formula-style electric race car while staying within a strict budget. Competing annually at Formula SAE Michigan, we go head-to-head with top universities across North America, showcasing our innovation in vehicle design, build quality, and team operations. With seven technical and three business departments, we offer hands-on experience in everything from aerodynamics and embedded software to sponsorship and logistics.
