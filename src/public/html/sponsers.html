Why our team needs your support

Sponsoring Ontario Tech Racing means empowering the next generation of engineers and innovators. Our team of over 70 Engineering and Business students designs, manufactures, and markets a Formula-electric style race car, competing on the global stage at events like the FSAE Electric Competition in Michigan. Your support directly contributes to real-world learning experiences, cutting-edge automotive technology, and our mission to excel against teams with significantly larger budgets. In return, your brand gains prominent exposure on our vehicle, merchandise, and social media, as well as at high-profile events such as Drive Festival and the FEL Cup. Join us on this journey to an electric future and help drive innovation forward. Contact us at motorsports@ontariotechu.net to learn more.
